Potato Wedges

Description

Potato wedges are another form of fried potatos. It is usually cut into thirds and you can bake it. Really simple.

Ingredients for 1 serving

  • 3 Russet Potatos
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Smoked paprika
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash and cut potato into thirds
  2. Put potatos in water for 30 minutes
  3. Preheat the oven at 400F
  4. Dry the potatos
  5. Add in the seasonings
  6. Add oilve oil and coat every potato
  7. Add potato into the oven and bake for 20 minutes
